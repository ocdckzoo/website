About The Band

Michigan's Premier
AC/DC Tribute

OCD/C is a dedicated tribute to the rawest, most dangerous era of AC/DC — the Bon Scott years. From the gritty swagger of High Voltage to the untamed fury of Highway to Hell, we bring the original spirit of rock and roll back to stages across Michigan and the Midwest.

OCD/C is deditated to the sound of that era, we may not look like them, but we sound like them! Every note, every riff, every scream is delivered with the same white-knuckle energy that made AC/DC legends. Whether you're a lifelong fan or hearing it for the first time, you'll feel it in your bones.
